About Our Clinic

Your trusted veterinary partner in Dewas for comprehensive pet care

Your Trusted Veterinary Partner in Dewas

Life Line Pet Clinic in Dewas is dedicated to providing exceptional veterinary care for all your beloved pets. Our clinic specializes in treating dogs, cats, parrots, rabbits, and tortoises with compassion and expertise.

With our 24-hour availability, your pets receive the care they need whenever they need it. Our experienced veterinarians are committed to the health and well-being of your furry and feathered family members.

We understand that your pets are family, and we treat them with the same care and attention we would give to our own. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern diagnostic and treatment equipment to ensure the best possible care for your pets.
